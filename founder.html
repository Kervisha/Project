About The Founder

Hi, I'm Kervisha Cordice a writer, author, singer/songwriter, visual artist, and founder of ButterflyBard Branding. My work is centered around my life as well as the lives of those I encounter thus often reflecting the human experience, mental health, and spirituality.

I graduated from The University of the West Indies in 2019 with a BA in Literatures in English and Linguistics as a Double Major. A major I chose by closing my eyes, tapping and hoping for the best. Now I'm an MEd student *fingers crossed*.

I was published in the IGDS 2018 Gender Studies journal where they featured my spoken-word poem, "Never Asking for It." However, my first book was a self-published collection of pictures, poems, and short stories entitled, "Sunrise on the Quadrangle". If you're interested in having a reading some of my work, you should check out the tab "Writings". If you do, I hope you enjoy it.

All I really want from my body of work is to know that it leaves just one person feeling less alone in their human experience, that is my motivation to create.

Thank you for reading.
Stay Groovy.
